01. About

Hey — I'm Dipesh, a frontend developer based in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, currently studying Computer Science at Pandit Deendayal Energy University. I chase the intersection of clean engineering and visual craft.

I believe great interfaces should feel obvious — no tutorials required. I obsess over interaction states, loading skeletons, and the micro-moments that make a product feel alive rather than just functional.
